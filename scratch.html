Why I Enjoy Skiing

Skiing is one of my favorite things to do because it combines speed, competition, and being outdoors. I like the challenge of getting better, pushing myself, and enjoying the mountains at the same time.

What I Like Most About Skiing

  1. Best parts of skiing
    • Going fast and improving technique
    • Being in the mountains with friends
    • Competing with myself to get better each season
  2. Trying different runs and terrain
  3. Enjoying winter and staying active
